Cape town

Cape town is my favorite city, and also the city I grew up in. The city centre is about a 30min drive from my house and is an international hotspot. The city is known for its harbor and its large array of fauna. Cape town is South Africa's oldest city and its 2nd largest! It is my favorite because of its scenic views, such as table mountain, its attractions, such as the waterfront, and the vibes of the people there.
