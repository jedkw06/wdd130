Western Cape

Table Mountain is a unique landmark and a great tourist attraction in Cape Town, Western Cape. It is a great mountain with is shape oddly and resembles a table due to the top being flat. The city of Cape Town is at the base of this mountain. Another famous mountain known as Lion's Head is located right next to table mountain, only adding to the attraction.
